Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I book a bus ticket on ADIBUS?

To book a bus ticket: 1) Enter your departure and destination cities, 2) Select your travel date, 3) Choose the number of passengers, 4) Click "Search Buses", 5) Select your preferred bus and seats, 6) Enter passenger details, 7) Make payment. You'll receive your ticket via email and SMS.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods including Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), Debit Cards, Net Banking, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and digital wallets. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

How can I cancel my bus ticket?

You can cancel your ticket by: 1) Going to "Cancellation" section under Bus Tickets section, 2) Enter your ticket number and mobile number, 3) Select the ticket you want to cancel, 4) Choose passengers to cancel, 5) Confirm cancellation. Refund will be processed according to the cancellation policy.

Can I change my travel date after booking?

Yes, you can change your travel date subject to availability and operator policies. Go to "Change Travel Date" section, enter your ticket details, select new date, and pay any fare difference. Some operators may charge a modification fee.

How long does it take to get a refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days for online payments. The exact time depends on your bank and payment method. For cash payments, refunds may take 7-10 business days. You'll receive SMS and email notifications about refund status.

What should I carry while traveling?

Always carry: 1) Valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, Passport), 2) Your bus ticket (printed or mobile), 3) Boarding pass if provided, 4) Any medical certificates if required. Arrive at the boarding point at least 15 minutes before departure.

Can I book tickets for someone else?

Yes, you can book tickets for family and friends. Just enter their correct details during booking. The passenger must carry valid ID proof matching the name on the ticket. You can book up to 6 passengers in a single transaction.

What if my bus is delayed or cancelled?

In case of delays or cancellations: 1) You'll be notified via SMS/email, 2) For delays over 2 hours, you can get a full refund, 3) For cancellations, automatic refund is processed, 4) Contact our support team for immediate assistance and alternative arrangements.
